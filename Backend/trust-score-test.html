🔍 What Trust Score REALLY Measures

Credibility & Confidence, NOT Intelligence

The Core Concept

Trust Score answers ONE question:
"How confident are we that this score is genuine?"

It is NOT:

  • ❌ A measure of intelligence
  • ❌ A measure of smartness
  • ❌ A measure of actual ability
  • ❌ Subject to interpretation

It IS:

  • ✅ A measure of credibility
  • ✅ A measure of authenticity
  • ✅ A measure of confidence in the score
  • ✅ A measure of whether the score is real vs lucky

The Formula

Trust Score = (Score Confidence + Percentile Confidence) / 2

Where:
• Score Confidence = min(normalizedScore, 100)
  = How much the raw score itself is trustworthy

• Percentile Confidence = percentile ≥ 50 ? 100 : (percentile × 2)
  = How much the ranking position is trustworthy

Result: 0-100% confidence level

Why It Matters

Two users can have the SAME score (say 80/100), but DIFFERENT trust levels:

Scenario Score Percentile Trust Score Why Different?
Scenario A 80/100 95% (Top 5%) 96% Score is consistent with high ranking = CREDIBLE
Scenario B 80/100 20% (Bottom 80%) 50% Score doesn't match ranking = ANOMALY
Scenario C 80/100 1% (Bottom 99%) 41% Score way too high for ranking = SUSPICIOUS
