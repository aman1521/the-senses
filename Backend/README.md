# 🚀 THE SENSES — PROJECT SUMMARY

**The Senses** is a **competitive intelligence and professional ranking platform** that measures real performance through controlled challenges and converts it into **trusted, explainable rankings** across global and local contexts.

Unlike traditional quizzes or resume-based evaluation, *The Senses* focuses on **how people think, decide, and perform under constraints**, while maintaining strict fairness, integrity, and transparency.

---

## 🎯 CORE OBJECTIVE

To create a **credible, performance-based professional identity system** where individuals can:

* Compete fairly within their professional profile
* Earn a global and local rank
* Track long-term growth and seasonal performance
* Be discoverable by employers through verified rankings

---

## 🧠 KEY FEATURES (FULL LIST)

---

### 1️⃣ Profile-Based Competition

* Users compete only within their chosen professional domain
  (e.g. Digital Marketer, Developer, Designer)
* Ensures **fair and comparable rankings**
* Prevents cross-domain score inflation

---

### 2️⃣ Secure Server-Side Scoring

* Questions and answers are validated on the server
* Client never sends scores
* One active session per user
* Session expiry and replay protection

**Result:** Cheating via client manipulation is eliminated.

---

### 3️⃣ Normalized Scoring System

Scores are calculated using:

* Accuracy
* Question difficulty
* Time behavior
* Completion integrity

**Result:** Rankings reflect true decision quality, not speed spamming or luck.

---

### 4️⃣ Absolute Global & Local Rankings

* Global rank within profile (e.g. #5 / 150)
* City-based ranking
* Company-based ranking
* College-based ranking

Ranks are:

* Absolute (not percentile)
* Calculated dynamically
* Always up-to-date

---

### 5️⃣ Best Score Ever + Seasonal Competition

* Lifetime best score is preserved
* Seasonal score resets with controlled decay
* Encourages long-term improvement and ongoing competition

---

### 6️⃣ Trust & Integrity System

Each user has:

* Trust level (high / medium / low)
* Human-likeness score
* Behavior-based integrity signals

Low-trust users are:

* Silently excluded from rankings
* Not publicly penalized
* Allowed to continue playing and recover trust

---

### 7️⃣ Anti-Cheat & Anti-Smurf Protection

* Detects abnormal timing patterns
* Detects repeated perfect accuracy
* Identifies multi-account behavior
* Prevents score farming and rank manipulation

No bans, no accusations — only **value exclusion**.

---

### 8️⃣ Human-Likeness Modeling

* Measures variance in response times
* Detects hesitation vs difficulty
* Tracks learning curves over time
* Identifies bot-like or assisted behavior

This makes the system **hard to game and easy to trust**.

---

### 9️⃣ Elite League System

* Top performers qualify for Elite League
* Elite membership is seasonal and defendable
* Promotion and relegation mechanics
* Elite results are employer-visible

**Elite status is earned, not permanent.**

---

### 🔟 Elite-Only Challenges & Tournaments

* Weekly elite challenges
* Seasonal elite tournaments
* Invite-only duels

These produce:

* High-trust competitive signals
* Prestige badges
* Legacy achievements

---

### 1️⃣1️⃣ Explainable Rankings (User-Facing)

Users can see:

* Why their rank moved up or down
* What signals are healthy or need attention
* Clear, non-technical explanations

No formulas or thresholds are exposed.

---

### 1️⃣2️⃣ Rank Storytelling (“How You Climbed”)

Each user gets a narrative timeline:

* Starting rank
* Key improvements
* Breakthrough moments
* Current standing

This builds emotional engagement and long-term retention.

---

### 1️⃣3️⃣ Personalized Improvement Recommendations

Users receive actionable guidance based on:

* Skill depth
* Decision quality
* Consistency
* Trust signals
* Elite eligibility

Recommendations are:

* Non-accusatory
* Behavioral
* Rank-oriented

---

### 1️⃣4️⃣ Verification System

* College verification
* Company verification
* Verified users gain access to:

  * Local leaderboards
  * Elite competition
  * Employer visibility

---

### 1️⃣5️⃣ Employer-Safe Rankings

Employers can:

* Discover top-ranked candidates
* Filter by profile, city, or company
* See ranks, badges, and elite status

They never see:

* Trust penalties
* Anti-cheat signals
* Appeals or negative history

---

### 1️⃣6️⃣ Audit & Compliance Readiness

* All integrity actions are logged internally
* Neutral language used for audit safety
* User consent is explicit
* Ranking disclaimers included

This makes the platform **legally defensible and enterprise-ready**.

---

## 🏗️ TECHNICAL STACK

* **Backend:** Node.js, Express
* **Database:** MongoDB (Mongoose)
* **Caching:** Redis (leaderboards)
* **Auth:** JWT
* **Architecture:** Modular MVC + Service Layer
* **Deployment Ready:** Linux, Docker, Cloud
